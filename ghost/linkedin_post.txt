[CVPR Findings 2026] Really happy to share that our paper "GHOST: Fast Category-agnostic Hand-Object Interaction Reconstruction from RGB Videos using Gaussian Splatting" has been accepted! 🎉

In this work, we tackle the challenge of reconstructing realistic hand-object interactions from a single RGB video — without requiring object templates or depth sensors. Using 2D Gaussian Splatting, GHOST achieves complete, physically consistent, and animatable reconstructions while running 13-16x faster than prior category-agnostic methods.

Our approach also won 🏆 1st place at the HANDS@ICCV25 workshop on the ARCTIC Bi-CAIR benchmark!
